About LensCore

Founded on
Intolerance
of Compromise

LensCore was established by optical engineers and professional photographers who were dissatisfied with the concessions made in every existing imaging system. We set out to build cameras without compromise — regardless of cost, timeline or convention.

Every product is designed, prototyped, tested and manufactured in our facility in Kyoto. Our engineers and our artists share the same corridors. The result is technology that understands what it means to capture light with purpose.

Components In-House
  • 01
    Uncompromising Quality
    We ship no product until it exceeds every specification in our internal quality charter. Production timelines are extended before quality standards are relaxed.
  • 02
    Optical Purity
    Every lens element is individually inspected. Sensor assemblies are tested at the pixel level. We do not tolerate sample variation — every unit performs to the same measured standard.
  • 03
    Built to Last
    LensCore cameras are designed for a minimum service life of fifteen years. Firmware, service parts and factory support are committed for every product for a decade after launch.
  • 04
    Responsible Manufacturing
    Carbon-neutral production since 2022. Conflict-free rare earth sourcing verified by third-party audit. Packaging is 100% recycled and recyclable.
  • 05
    The Photographer's Vision
    Every product decision is reviewed by our panel of twelve working professionals spanning photojournalism, fine art, wildlife and cinema — before it reaches a factory floor.
